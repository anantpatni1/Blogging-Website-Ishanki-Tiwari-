11:11

Posted on June 13, 2017

Have you ever been said, “Hey! It’s 11:11. Make a wish”.

Does 11:11 have some sort of magical wishing power in it?

Then why do people firmly believe in it? I was never a 11:11 person, until and unless, my friend once told me. She closed her eyes and made a wish.

When I glimpsed at her, I thought may be there is something due to which people have faith in this. Recently, in February, her happiness made me realize that whatever she aspired for happened to be true. What if I desire for something, and it won’t get accomplished? Then I would never be able to yearn for something.

I researched for it everywhere, its significance, its meaning. But nothing was as clear as I wanted. So, for a trial, I tried wishing for something. I didn’t desire for something very massive but a small little urge. And it did come true. Really, it’s in line with keeping your thoughts focused positively towards what you want to manifest. 11:11 is a reminder that our thoughts are manifesting… When you see this number focus on what you want!

Wake up and take control of consciously creating blessings in your life!

11:11 is a beautiful reminder of the synchronicity present, when we are following our hearts, and moving forward in life with love.

I later comprehended that it is just a belief in yourself, in the positivity around you, and if you’re seeing this number repeatedly then the Universe is giving some hints to you.

Follow that hint and credence your thoughts. When you see 11:11, you should simply stop and recognize the significance of the moment.

In my opinion, and what resonates with me, is that when you see 1111, whether on a clock, a license plate, your bank statement or anywhere else, it’s is like a friendly little sign from your soul, source, higher self, God, or the entire Universe that everything is in alignment and you’re exactly where you need to be.

I have no idea that whether we will ever be able to identify what it means but you can decide for yourself what it means to you.

I know I always get a smile on my face when I see it; and now I even share it with others, “Hey! Look it’s 11:11”.

11:11 is a sign there is greater Light and Love, available to you…Open your heart, Quiet your mind and Tune in!

Ishanki Tiwari
