Teepee Night.

Posted on July 5, 2016

Day 5:

As Edward lacked in communication but the journey of their LOVE train had enough diesel in it to run for a lifetime. Scarlett proposed him without giving a second chance to her decision. And by this Edward was on cloud nine to have her in his life. By the time Scarlett became his preceptor to make him a good conversationalist. And when one introvert and one extrovert are in love, one of the greatest paradoxes of life happens, one of the most beautiful phenomenon: They are together yet tremendously alone; they are together so much that they’re almost one. But their oneness doesn’t destroy their individuality, in fact, it enhances it.

Now Edward achieved perfection in expressing himself. And he passed his test with flying colors by convincing Scarlett to sojourn for one more week with him in North Hampton.

Day 6:

Now Scarlett was living with Edward in his apartment, in his house. In the meanwhile Edward had already planned to make his new love and this whole 1 week a special journey. They enjoyed each and every moment together by Watching movies, going on dates, having all types of cuisine and beverages. On the night of the fourth day he gifted a beautiful dress, a pair of stilettos and a bouquet to his princess by telling her the innermost feelings of his heart.

“When I’m with you, I act different. In a good way. I smile more and laugh more. I don’t have to pretend everything is okay when it’s really not. With you, I can drop the fake smile and put a real one. I don’t feel hurt and alone when I’m with you, instead I feel loved. You’re easy to talk to and you, listen to me. You show me that you really do care and not just pretending about it. I really appreciate your company, because with you I am different. With you I’m happy.”

He wasn’t just astounding but he has organized a very big flabbergast for her the very next day. Scarlett was all in tears. She has never foreseen him to be this admirable in articulating himself.

Day 7:

Next day starts with Good Morning kisses on a special cup of coffee. On that Eve Edward took her to the candle light dinner and then what Scarlett saw was just pleasing her eyes so much. Nearby Edward had prepared a teepee and besoothed it with cushions, lighting and duvet all around. After dinner he took her to that teepee and they talked about everything that night. He asked her to talk to her parents about their marriage when she will return to Bradford. Though he didn’t want her to go. The night continued by making love, but that love wasn’t a concupiscence, it was the sign of commitment towards each other. The sign of extreme care. The sign, showering that kind of love which cannot be expressed in words.

Day after day, the whole week was over. It was time for Scarlett to leave. He came to drop her till the airport. Both had dewy-eyed. Their mind were struggling with words but they didn’t speak much. Because adieus were never so painless. *Edward had no idea what kind of volcano was planning to destroy his life*
