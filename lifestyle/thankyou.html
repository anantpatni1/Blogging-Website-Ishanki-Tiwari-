  • For being such a wonderful welcoming host to my friends of anywhere. The 12-year-old me observed the significance of acceptance of people across boundaries.

  • For being critical and careful with your praises towards my high grades and other successes. It helped the 13-year-old me to stay grounded and humble.

  • For cracking all your never ending series of poor jokes, puns and situational humour. The 14-year-old me inculcated a good sense of humour thanks to her cool dad.
